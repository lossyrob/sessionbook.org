SessionBook

Irish trad chord charts, done right.

A home for accompanists. Author chord charts, organize them into sets, assemble session sheets, and print them in a format that works on a music stand.

  • Chord charts you can write by hand and print cleanly
  • Sets and gig sheets as first-class objects
  • Pinned revisions — your gig sheet never changes under you
  • Built for guitar, bouzouki, piano, and keys
